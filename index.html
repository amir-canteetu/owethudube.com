Meet Owethu Dube, the dynamo born to light up stages and screens alike! Hailing from the vibrant tapestry of South Africa, this rising starlet now calls Edmonton, Alberta home sweet home.

Picture this: a fusion of South African spice and Canadian cool, brewed to perfection in the heart of Owethu's artistic upbringing. From the tender age of 15 months, she's been soaking up the best of both worlds, nurtured in a household buzzing with creativity and curiosity.

But wait, there's more! Catch her dazzling debut in the Explore Edmonton and Travel Alberta Commercial, "This Is The Game," celebrating the city's passion for inclusive sports. When she's not stealing scenes, Owethu's honing her craft at the LeBlanc School of Acting, where every class feels like a curtain call.

But acting's just the tip of the iceberg for this multi-talented maven. She struts her stuff on the catwalk, dives into improv with gusto, and takes flight as an aerialist, spinning stories with every graceful twirl. And let's not forget her flair for fashion – she stitches together her dreams one design at a time, turning heads with her signature style.

Offstage, Owethu's a force of nature, zipping around on her hoverboard or pedaling through the neighborhood on her trusty bike. With her faithful sidekick Pluto the Shitzu by her side, every adventure is an epic tale waiting to unfold.

But the world isn't just a stage for Owethu – it's a playground, a canvas, a symphony of sights and sounds waiting to be explored. From sampling exotic cuisines to soaking in sunsets on far-flung shores, she's a globetrotter with an insatiable thirst for adventure.

And the party doesn't stop when the curtain falls – not with Owethu lighting up screens and feeds with her infectious energy. From TikTok antics to Instagram escapades, she's a digital diva with a heart of gold, spreading joy and inspiration to fans around the globe.

So buckle up, folks – Owethu Dube's just getting started, and the world ain't seen nothing yet. With her star on the rise and her spirit soaring high, the best is yet to come in this blockbuster tale of talent, tenacity, and triumph!
